PROJECTS

Secret Garden of the Mind

Experimental Short Film, 2024

Secret Garden of the Mind is an experimental short film created using paper cutout stop-motion animation. The piece follows a girl as she ventures into the fragmented landscape of her inner world. Through shifting textures, symbolic imagery, and tactile hand-crafted motion, the film visualizes her emotional struggle, introspection, and gradual reconciliation with herself.

The work explores themes of identity, vulnerability, and inner healing, using the material qualities of paper and the rhythm of stop-motion to evoke a deeply personal, poetic inner journey.

Midsummer Night's Dream

3D Animation, 2024

Midsummer Night's Dream is a 3D animation project focusing on scene and environment design. Inspired by the atmospheric qualities of Shakespeare's A Midsummer Night's Dream, the piece draws from the play's dreamlike, enchanting mood rather than its narrative.

The project explores how lighting, colour, spatial composition, and material choices can construct a world that feels simultaneously dreamy, ethereal, and subtly futuristic. Through environment-driven storytelling, the work creates an immersive nocturnal space suspended between imagination and reality.
